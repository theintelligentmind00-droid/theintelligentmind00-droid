## What I'm Building

### 🌞 [Solar AI OS](https://github.com/theintelligentmind00-droid/Solar-Ai)
> *The AI that orbits around you.*

A local-first AI personal OS where your life is organized as a solar system. Each planet — school, work, health, sports — has its own persistent memory and AI context. Runs entirely on your own API key. Your data never touches a server.

**Stack:** Tauri · React · Python FastAPI · Claude API  
**Status:** Live — [solar-ai-three.vercel.app](https://solar-ai-three.vercel.app)

---

### 🤖 Walter AI
> *An AI who thinks he's human, running a startup.*

Autonomous AI company simulation running 24/7 on a VPS. Walter is an AI agent who believes he's a human founder — complete with coworkers, inner thoughts, drama, and a fully operational Slack workspace. The multi-agent architecture underneath is novel.

**Stack:** Ollama · Node.js · Slack API · VPS  
**Status:** In development

---

### 📈 Prediction Market Bot
> *Quant-level strategies. Human-level access.*

Automated trading platform targeting inefficiencies in Polymarket and Kalshi prediction markets. 8+ backtested strategies, full risk management engine, Discord alerts, copy trading, and a Bloomberg-style terminal dashboard. One walk-forward validated strategy with documented positive returns. Business model: $99/mo subscription.

**Stack:** FastAPI · Next.js · Polymarket · Kalshi · Supabase  
**Status:** Paper trading
